How It Works

Our program is designed to be flexible, engaging, and easy to integrate into student life.

Delivery Modes

In-School Sessions

Conducted directly in schools during curriculum or extracurricular time. Designed to complement academic learning.

Weekend Workshops

Fun, interactive weekend sessions for parents and students to learn together — great for after-school enrichment.

Online Courses (Coming Soon)

Self-paced video lessons with printable worksheets and live Q&A sessions. Accessible anytime, anywhere.

What Each Lesson Includes

  1. 1

    Story

    Real-life scenarios that help students relate and connect emotionally.

  2. 2

    Group Discussion

    Guided conversations that encourage expression and critical thinking.

  3. 3

    Activity

    Hands-on tasks like role plays, games, or creative projects.

  4. 4

    Reflection

    Thought-provoking questions to apply learning to real life.

  5. 5

    Feedback

    Constructive input from facilitators to support growth and improvement.
