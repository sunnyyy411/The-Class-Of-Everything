Why Life Skills Matter

Children are taught to solve math problems, but not how to manage emotions.
They're tested on grammar, but not on how to talk to strangers or handle stress.

The Real-Life Gap

India's education system produces exam toppers who struggle with real life. While schools focus on textbooks, these critical life skills are being missed:

1. Financial Literacy

76% of Indian teens can't budget pocket money (NCFE 2023)

We teach saving, investing and scam prevention - because money skills shouldn't be a mystery

2. Communication Skills

56% of graduates lack confidence in speaking (India Skills Report 2024)

We turn shy speakers into storytellers - because your voice matters more than marks

3. Critical Thinking

Only 12% of Class 8 students can solve logic puzzles (ASER 2023)

We train problem-solvers, not memorizers - because the future belongs to innovators

4. Emotional Intelligence

42% of teens show anxiety signs (NCERT 2023)

We build coping skills - because mental health is as vital as math

5. Digital Safety

62% face cyberbullying, 90% stay silent (CyberPeace 2024)

We make the digital world safer - because the internet shouldn't be scary

6. Health & First Aid

80% schools lack first-aid training (Indian Red Cross 2023)

We teach lifesaving basics - because accidents happen to everyone

7. Civic Values

60% can't name fundamental rights (CSDS 2023)

We foster responsibility - because good citizens are made, not born

8. Study & Tech Skills

78% use AI, 52% plagiarize (DU 2024)

We show how to learn smart - because Google has answers, but you need questions

9. DIY & Vocational Skills

92% parents want kids to learn cooking/repairs (ParentCircle 2024)

We make kids self-reliant - because adulthood shouldn't begin with YouTube tutorials

10. Career Exploration

74% pick careers under parental pressure (Univariety 2024)

We help discover true passions - because doing what you love beats prestige

11. Experiential Learning

Kids retain 90% by doing (NEP 2020)

We replace rote learning with hands-on projects - because life isn't multiple choice

12. Future Readiness

58% schools ignore AI/sustainability (STEM.org 2024)

We prep for tomorrow's jobs today - because the future won't wait
